About Me

A little about myself...

BIO

I recently purchased a home in Liberty Hill that sits on half of an acre in the downtown area and I love it! Being able to get the small town vibe, but still having access to the big city is the ideal balance for me. Before getting into web development, I was in ministry for about 10 years where I worked as a worship pastor and tech coordinator. I learned the value of being able to provide efficient technical instruction, oversight, and leadership. Although ministry and web development don't appear to have much overlap, I've learned that being a part of a highly collaborative team requires the use of many of the skills I learned while in ministry. I've thoroughly enjoyed the transition into web development and the ever-evolving technical expertise required to stay up to date in this industry. I love to learn and I love to help others do the same!
